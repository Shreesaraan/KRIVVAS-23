Event Guideline

Hola amigos!
Krivvas'23, the annual cultural fest of Sona, is back again and better than ever before. Also, yes, it's offline. We, the students of Sona-Tech, conduct Krivvas every year and made sure last time's offline event was enthralling for all its participants. Now the wait is over. With just the perfect mix of cultural events, we plan on making Krivvas'23 an unforgettable experience for you. Trust us when we say that you won't miss out on anything. Check out our website, as it has answers to all the questions that you might have. Stay tuned and brace yourselves for the journey of a lifetime with Krivvas'23!
